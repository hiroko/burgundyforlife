What is Burgundy for Life?

Burgundy for Life is a collection of advocacy initiatives by Hiroko Nishimura to promote Vascular Malformation Awareness.

We provide resources and support for survivors and their loved ones by harnessing the power of social media and the internet.

The various initiatives focus on Arteriovenous Malformations, Brain Aneurysms, Brain Injuries, and Vascular Bleeds.
