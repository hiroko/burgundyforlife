What is Burgundy for Life?

Burgundy for Life is a collection of advocacy initiatives by Hiroko Nishimura to promote Vascular Malformation Awareness. We provide resources and support for survivors and their loved ones.

The various initiatives focus on Arteriovenous Malformation, Brain Aneurysm, Brain Injury, and Stroke (and other Vascular Bleeds).

Other areas of interests are Disability Inclusion and Community (Re)habilitation.
